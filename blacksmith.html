A 2D top down action and blacksmithing game made with a group of 4 U of Michigan students: Ian Cook, Hussain Lokhandwala, Rushabh Shah, and myself. I handled the combat side of the game, working on all enemy types and player weapons and abilities. I also made the artwork for the enemies and designed all the particle effects and difficulty scaling.
